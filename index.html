About Bo

Born, raised, and based in Las Vegas since 1979, Bo Wiltse has been immersed in the world of art for as long as he can remember. He started his journey as a graffiti artist in 1993, later expanding his craft to tattooing in 2004. With a lifelong passion for creativity, Bo works across multiple mediums, including spray paint, brushes, airbrush, digital illustration, 3D modeling, and sculpting.

Beyond art, Bo is a proud single father of five amazing children, who are his greatest inspiration. His youngest son, at just 12 years old, even built this website—proof that creativity runs in the family. Art is also a family affair, as Bo works alongside his two talented sisters, who share his passion for tattooing and creativity.

Over the years, Bo has had the honor of working with many well-known clients, including professional athletes and other public figures. His work has also been featured at several major events in his hometown of Las Vegas, further cementing his reputation as a versatile and innovative artist.

Bo's artistic journey has been shaped by the support and mentorship of several close friends who guided him along the way. Their influence, along with his sisters, continues to inspire his work, pushing him to create original and meaningful pieces for his clients.

If you're looking for an artist who can bring your vision to life in a truly one-of-a-kind way, Bo Wiltse is the creator for you.
