About Me

Born and raised in Las Vegas since 1979, I have been immersed in the world of art for as long as I can remember. I started my journey as a graffiti artist in 1993, later expanding my craft to tattooing in 2004.
With a lifelong passion for creativity, I work across multiple mediums, including spray paint, brushes, airbrush, digital illustration, 3D modeling, and sculpting. Beyond art, I am a proud single father of five amazing children, who are my greatest inspiration. My youngest, at just 11 years old, even built this website, proof that creativity runs in the family.(Source code in footer!)

Art is also a family affair, as I work alongside my two talented sisters, who share my passion for tattooing and creativity. Over the years, I have had the honor of working with numerous well-known clients, including professional athletes and other public figures.
My work has also been featured at several major events in my hometown of Las Vegas, further cementing my reputation as a versatile and innovative artist. My artistic journey has been shaped by the support and mentorship of several close friends who guided me along the way.
